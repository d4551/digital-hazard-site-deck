Project Cobalt Overview:
- Isometric, brawler-esque gameplay.
- Punchy electronic and metal soundtrack that complements fast-paced combat.
- Comic book-inspired art style packed with VFX, word splashes, and streamable “wow” moments.
- Modular story packs via the unique Anchor System.
- Live-service DLC model with new characters and ongoing content.

Project Cobalt delivers a multiplayer squad-building experience inspired by Helldivers 2 and Judge Dredd. The game combines chaotic multiplayer combat with a modular character system and a distinctive comic art style.

Unique Selling Points (USP):
1. Multiplayer Squad-Builder
2. Flashy, Punchy Gameplay
3. The Anchor System

These key differentiators blend familiar mechanics with new innovations to create stylistic crossover appeal.

---

Multiplayer Squad-Builder:
- Emphasizes teamwork, chaos, and strategic upgrades.
- Online Play and Couch Co-Op modes.
- RPG-inspired squad variety and progression.
- Semi-permanent upgrades create meaningful choices.

Players experiment to build the perfect squad, balancing casual fun and strategic depth.

---

Flashy, Punchy Gameplay:
- Designed to be as fun to watch as it is to play.
- Combines power fantasy, large-scale combat, and over-the-top visuals.
- Heroes unleash massive VFX, ultimates, and squad synergy attacks.
- Constant action with no downtime – teamwork is key.

---

The Anchor System:
- Each run tailored to a specific Anchor character and squad.
- Modular stories allow standalone packs with unique environments and enemies.
- The “Lend Your Might” mechanic enables super-charged ultimate abilities.

Market Impact:
- Modular DLC structure allows collaborations with external IPs.
- Enables flexible storytelling without affecting source IPs.
- Targets broad, data-driven player segments.

---

Comic Art Stylings:
- Hand-animated characters and enemies give a stylized comic-book look.
- Visual inspirations: Ghost in the Shell, Hi-Fi Rush, Persona 5 Royal, Teen Titans, FLCL.

==== PROJECT HAZAI ====

Project HazAI – The Next Frontier in AI-Enhanced Tools for Games, Media, & Entertainment

---

The Team:
- Erick Barnes – CEO
- Rodolfo Segleau – Product
- Sean Van Osselaer – Engineering & Tech
- Roland Li – Machine Learning

---

Company Vision:
Empowering artists to push creative boundaries and elevate audience experiences using state-of-the-art AI tools.

---

Mission Statement:
To pioneer a transformative, human-centered approach to AI-powered workflows that solve the toughest challenges in Games, Media, and Entertainment production.

---

The Problem:
- No robust AI tools exist for the professional 2D space.
- 2D animation requires extensive manual redrawing.
- Current AI tools lack production-level control.
- Poor integration with industry-standard tools.

---

The Solution:
- A platform evolving alongside studio workflows.
- Deep integration with industry standards (Toon Boom, Moho, TVPaint, Unity, Unreal).
- Artists retain granular control over visuals.

Goal: Enhance, not replace, artistic expression while optimizing efficiency.

---

Market Size:
- Animation Software Market: $155B (Worldwide), $56B (U.S.)
- 2D Animation Market: $39B (Worldwide), $14B (U.S.)
- CAGR: 3-8%
- Target Markets: North America, Asia, Europe
- Projected attach rate: 1-4%

---

Go-To-Market Strategy:
**Target Users:**  
- 2D game developers & co-dev studios  
- 2D animation and media production houses  
- Outsourcing & support studios  

**Integration Strategy:**  
- Attach to Unity/Unreal (raster-based outputs)
- Attach to Toon Boom, Moho, TVPaint (vector-based outputs)

**Why It Works:**  
- Minimal workflow disruption
- Seamless adoption via familiar tools
- Simplified pipelines, consistent output, and boosted production velocity

---

Roadmap:
**Phase 1 – Proof of Concept:**  
- Validate AI-assisted 2D animation via Digital Hazard’s in-house studio.

**Phase 2 – MVP (v1.0):**  
- Standalone tool with industry-standard export support.
- Studio/project-specific training models.
- Intelligent rigging, pose control, and animation transfer.

**Phase 3 – Enhanced Functionality:**  
- Deep integrations with leading tools.
- Proprietary hosted AI models for licensing.
- Expansion of IP and partner integrations.
